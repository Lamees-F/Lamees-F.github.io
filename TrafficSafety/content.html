Road Safety...

  • Refers to the measures and practices that are put in place to prevent Accidents, Injuries and Fatalites on the road.
  • In another meaning is actions performed to lower the risk of fatalities and injuries from traffic accidents.

    • So for realizing the importance of roads, the Saudi Government constructed high quality networks of roads all over the country.


  • Building side parking.
  • Painting the middle and the sides of roads.
  • Widening the bridges, equal to the road and its shoulders.
  • Providing the road with reflector marks (cat's eye).
